INTRODUCTION

At Magnolia, we see content as more than deliverables. Every piece of content is a creative expression, and every campaign is an opportunity to build a world.

We design 360 campaigns with a social-first lens, creative ideas that are conceived to live cohesively across platforms. From hero moments to cultural touchpoints. We develop creative system that allow a single campaign idea to unfold, evolve, and express itself in many forms, on every platform, without ever losing its point of view.

We turn insights into creative strategy, creative strategy into visuals, and visuals into stories that feel elevated, entertaining, educational, aspirational, and culturally fluent.

This is what defines us as a partner. We understand that scaling creative should never dilute it, and our process is designed to ensure it never does.

We’d love to bring your vision to life and create something truly exceptional together.

drop us an email bonjour@maisonmagnolia.com

At Maison Magnolia, everything is on the menu.

In addition to full-scale 360 campaigns, we love rolling up our sleeves for ad hoc collaborations: whether it’s a one-off asset or a burst of content.

CURATION

Magnolia is a creative house and talent collective group. We believe craftsmanship and agility should coexist in luxury. Every touchpoint, from a hero billboard to a six-second film, is treated with the same level of intention and artistry.

DIRECTION

We are not just tastemakers. We are architects of ideas and narratives, building worlds that live far beyond a single post or frame.

EXECUTION

We work in agile, modular steps that keep strategy and creativity aligned from start to finish. We begin with a deep dive into your brand, shape concepts through rapid ideation, and produce with precision across photo, video and social formats.
